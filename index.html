Meet the BT21 Characters!

These lovable characters, created by BTS, travel across the galaxy to spread love! TATA, prince of Planet BT, with his guardian robot VAN travel to earth anf encounter six earthlings. Together, TATA, VAN, KOYA, RJ, SHOOKY, MANG, CHIMMY, and COOKY become the most influential pop-culture sensation in the galaxy. Now known as, BT21.


Meet TATA!


Tata, the crown prince of Planet BT possess supernatural powers and has a super elastic body!


Meet VAN!


Van, a knowledgeable robot protects everyone of BT21!


Meet KOYA!


Koya, a very talented and sleepy koala with detachable ears!


Meet RJ!

RJ, a fluffly llama that still manages to get cold, loves to cook and eat!

Meet SHOOKY!

Shooky, a cookie, loves pulling pranks but hates milk!


Meet MANG!

Mang, a dancer hides under a mask to keep his identity a secret!


Meet CHIMMY!

Chimmy, a dog in a yellow hoodie loves to work as hard as he can!


Meet COOKY!

Cooky, a pink bunny with uneven eyebrows and muscles is that one friend you can count on!
